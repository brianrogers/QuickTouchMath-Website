Quick Touch Math

QuickTouchMath is a math challenge game for the whole family. Easily add family and friends to the challenge list and see who has the quickest touch.

Choose from addition, subtraction, multiplication or division and see how many you can answer correctly in 60 seconds.

I wrote this app to help my kids with their 'math memory' and to help improve their confidence in school. I hope it is as fun and challenging for you as it is for our family.

Features:

  • Easily add your family and friends and challenge them.
  • Practice your 'Math Memory'.
  • Includes Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication and Division.
